Hemoglobin (Hb):
  Normal: 12.0–15.0 g/dL (female)
  Low: Suggests anemia
  High: Suggests dehydration or polycythemia

MCV (Mean Corpuscular Volume):
  Normal: 83–101 fl
  High: Macrocytic anemia (e.g., B12 or folate deficiency)
  Low: Microcytic anemia (e.g., iron deficiency)

Neutrophils:
  High: Possible infection or inflammation
  Low: May indicate bone marrow issue or immune suppression
